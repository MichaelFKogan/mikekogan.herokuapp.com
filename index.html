Services

What Can I Do For You?

Web Development

I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions. Having a responsive layout means that your website fluidly resizes for optimal viewing regardless of the screen size or device (e.g. iPhone, iPad).

Front-End Design

Front End Development is building out the visual components of a website. I enjoy using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, to bring ideas to life in the browser and create intuitive user experiences while ensuring simplicity and ease of use.

Mobile Game Development

Having a game idea and then being able to bring that idea to life can be a challenge. I've designed games from scratch including all artwork, music, and sound effects and have deployed several games to the App Store and Google Play Store.
